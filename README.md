Privacy Policy — Kontxt Chrome Extension
Last updated: March 30, 2026

1. Data We Collect
Kontxt does not collect, transmit, or store any user data on external servers.
All data — including annotations, skills, and settings — is stored locally in
your browser using Chrome's built-in storage APIs.

2. API Keys
If you choose to enable AI suggestions, you provide your own API key
(Anthropic, OpenAI, or Google Gemini). This key is stored locally in
chrome.storage.local and is never transmitted to anyone other than the
respective AI provider when you explicitly trigger AI suggestions.

3. Microphone Access
Voice recording uses Chrome's built-in SpeechRecognition API. Audio is
processed locally by your browser and sent to Google's speech recognition
service (as part of Chrome's built-in API). Kontxt does not record, store,
or transmit audio data.

4. Page Content
When AI suggestions are enabled and you annotate an element, the element's
label, type, and URL are sent to your chosen AI provider to generate
suggestions. No other page content is transmitted.

5. Third-Party Services
Kontxt communicates only with the AI provider you configure:
  - Anthropic (api.anthropic.com)
  - OpenAI (api.openai.com)
  - Google Gemini (generativelanguage.googleapis.com)
These are governed by their respective privacy policies.

6. Data Sharing
We do not sell, trade, or share any user data with third parties.

7. Contact
For questions about this privacy policy, contact: contact@aibhuyan.com
